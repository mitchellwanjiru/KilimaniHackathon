Current Issues in Kilimani

Kilimani faces various challenges that impact the daily lives of its residents. Here are some of the pressing issues:

  • Water Shortage: The area has been experiencing intermittent water supply, affecting households and businesses.
  • Green Space Reduction: Urban development has led to the reduction of green spaces, impacting the environment and quality of life.
  • Traffic Congestion: Increasing traffic has led to congestion, making commutes longer and more stressful.
  • Security Concerns: There have been reports of increased crime rates in certain parts of Kilimani, affecting the safety of residents.
