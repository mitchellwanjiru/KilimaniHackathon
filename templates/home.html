Know Kilimani

Kilimani is a vibrant neighborhood in Nairobi, Kenya, known for its diverse community and dynamic environment. It houses a sizable segment of the upper middle-income population and offers a mix of residential and commercial properties. The area is popular for its shopping centers, schools, and recreational facilities.

Community and Lifestyle

The community in Kilimani is diverse and cosmopolitan, with a mix of expatriates, professionals, and families. The area offers numerous amenities including shopping malls like Yaya Centre and Junction Mall, schools like Kilimani Primary School, and various restaurants and entertainment spots.
