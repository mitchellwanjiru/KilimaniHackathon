Explore the Water Situation

Kilimani, once known for its reliable water supply, now faces a growing water crisis. Here are some factors contributing to the problem:

  • Population Growth: Increased population has led to higher demand for water, outstripping supply.
  • Infrastructure Challenges: Aging infrastructure and inadequate maintenance have resulted in frequent water supply interruptions.
  • Environmental Changes: Changes in weather patterns and environmental degradation have affected water sources.

Efforts are being made to address these issues through infrastructure upgrades, water conservation initiatives, and community awareness programs.
